"Web-based On-the-Job Training Portal"

Welcome to the Web-based On-the-Job Training Portal for Bulacan State University! Our system streamlines the management of on-the-job training programs for students, HTE, and OJT instructors. Students can easily track their progress, while employers and OJT instructors can post opportunities, review applications, and monitor performance. Our user-friendly system is accessible from any device with an internet connection, making the OJT experience seamless and rewarding. Thank you for choosing our system for your OJT needs!
